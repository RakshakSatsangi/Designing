The following are the instructions that to be followed for playing our game:

1) You have to score goal by passing the ball. Scoring Goal has 5 points.
2) There is a host which creates a game and anothers can join it.
3) A player cannot hold a ball for more than 3 seconds. 
4) If the player holds it for more than 3 seconds, the ball is automatically released from the player.
5) The direction of the shoot is controlled by the mouse pointer.
